# **App Name**: SkinSmith Clinic Manager

## Core Features:

- Doctor Management: Add, edit, and delete doctors with detailed information including specialization, qualifications, consultation fees, and availability.
- Patient Management: Add patients by name, mobile number, age, and gender. Mobile number is used to uniquely identify and fetch patient history. Includes patient search functionality.
- Appointment Scheduling: Schedule appointments for doctors, manage walk-in patients, and track appointment status (waiting, in consultation, completed) on a daily dashboard.
- Visit Records: Record patient diagnoses, prescribed medicines, procedures, treatments, and doctor's notes for each visit, saved permanently to patient history.
- Inventory Management: Track medical and cosmetic products including product name, category, supplier, purchase price, selling price, quantity, and expiry date. Provides auto-updates for stock levels when products are prescribed or sold and low stock/expiry alerts.
- Billing and Payments: Generate bills for consultations, procedures, and medicines. Supports cash, UPI, and card payments, and generates printable invoices. Stores billing history per patient.
- Intelligent Recommendations Tool: Generative AI tool providing AI powered recommendations for products that could be marked for discounts, close to their expiration date.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#008080) to evoke trust and cleanliness.
- Background color: Light grayish-teal (#E0EEEE) for a soft, medical-themed backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted blue (#77B5FE) for highlighting key actions and information.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' for a modern, yet warm feel. This sans-serif will provide readability and a contemporary look for both headlines and body text.
- Use a set of clear, consistent icons for navigation and actions throughout the system. Icons should follow a flat design style.
- Design a clean and intuitive layout that minimizes clicks for daily operations, optimizing the experience for desktop and tablet use. Implement a clear visual hierarchy to guide users through tasks.
- Subtle transitions and feedback animations to enhance user interaction and provide clear visual cues.
